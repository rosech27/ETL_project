Population & Voting Trends (Arizona)

Rose Chakkoria & Haylye Plaster

This is a study of voting trends of Arizona and it's population demographics

Report

This website is made to display the ETL capabilities of Haylye Plaster and Rose Chakkoria. This site is made specifically to bring data together and tell a story. The data you see on the ETL project overview is comprised of historic data of Arizona’s population and Arizona political trends. We wanted to see if there was a correlation between population increase and voter behavior in general presidential elections.

Voting Data : https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arizona

Tech Stack

We took data from and censusscope.org to retrieve the data we needed. The only issue we saw with our data was the credibility of Wikipedia as a source, with that we cross-referenced it with a highly respected website usaelctionatlas.org to confirm the accuracy of the data. After confirming accuracy we used PANDAS to scrap the data from the two websites and transformed it into tables using PYTHON. To properly join the data sets we needed to transform the population and population by ethnicity to match the years in the political trend data table, we did this by utilizing the average function in EXCEL to produce election years in our population data.

Once all the data was transformed we choose to send our data to a MONGO DB in order to later use FLASK for the HTML portion to tell the story of Arizona’s population growth and political development. Furthermore, to show the data we decided on two charts to best show the data, creating one on political trends (line chart) and one on population growth, broken down by ethnicity(stacked bar chart).

Once the data was sent to a MONGO DB, we shifted to built out the bones of the HTML. We used bootstrap in order to get the HTML to fit our vision for how to best tell the story of Arizona’s population growth and political development. Through bootstrap, we were able to gain those bare bones and develop the web page further to best fit our needs. Furthermore, in order to display the data scraped from the websites above, it was necessary to create a Flask application which launched the HTML page.
